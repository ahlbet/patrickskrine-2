My name is Patrick Skrine. I’m from Oak Park, Illinois and an OPRF graduate, where I played basketball and football.

I am a communications major, hopeful to pursue a career in public relations, sports marketing, or possibly the teaching/coaching path. Communications is very versatile and can open many different doors. I’m most looking forward to developing my skills in the sports media world.

I am currently a sophomore at Triton College, but I will be transferring to a four-year university in the fall. I have contributed to two publications as a sports journalist: Notre Dame’s The Observer and the Oak Park Wednesday Journal, where I’m currently at.

The highlight of my experience as a reporter for the Wednesday Journal was my night in the NBA. I attended the Cleveland Cavaliers-Chicago Bulls game on Dec. 4, 2017 at the United Center. I observed the game from the press box, listened in on press conferences and conducted a 1-on-1 interview with Cavs G/F Kyle Korver.

Basketball has been my ultimate passion in sports. While I still have plans to continue my playing career, I have helped coach several different teams, I have volunteered at camps, and I train clients 1-on-1. Working with kids on and off the court has solidified my interest in being a teacher and coach.

Follow me here as I discuss all things sports and beyond. The experiences I’ve had and the people I’ve met have made my journey special so far.

Be kind, be grateful, laugh often and enjoy the ride.
